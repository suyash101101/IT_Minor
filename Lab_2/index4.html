Destination: Paris

Paris, the City of Lights, blends timeless art, stunning architecture, and cozy cafes. From walks along the Seine to sunsets at Montmartre, there is beauty in every corner.

Things to Do

  1. Visit the Eiffel Tower and Trocadéro gardens
  2. Enjoy a cruise on the Seine River
  3. Explore masterpieces at the Louvre Museum
